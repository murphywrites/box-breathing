It involves breathing in and out and holding the breath. It may help clear the mind, relax the body, and improve focus. p Box breathing involves four basic steps, each lasting 4 seconds: ul li breathing in li holding the breath li breathing out li holding the breath p Box breathing, also known as resetting the breath or four-square breathing, is easy to do, quick to learn, and can be highly effective in stressful situations. p People with high stress jobs, such as soldiers and police officers, often use box breathing when their bodies are in fight-or-flight mode.
